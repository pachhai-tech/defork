Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Short version: We don’t use cookies or track you across sites. The MVP uses cookie-free analytics (optional). Content you create stays client-side until you choose to upload it to IPFS and mint on-chain.

  • Wallets: Connections happen directly in your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase, or WalletConnect). We don’t store your keys.
  • Content: Uploads go to IPFS via Web3.Storage; hashes and URIs may appear on-chain if you mint.
  • Analytics (optional): If enabled, Plausible’s privacy-first analytics collect aggregate usage only—no cookies, no personal data.
  • Moderation: If you “report” a token, a small JSON is written to IPFS for community review.
  • Contact: Open an issue on GitHub for questions or takedown requests.
